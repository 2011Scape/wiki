Construction - Dungeon Rooms

Introduction | Key Features | Corridor and Junction Hotspots | Dungeon Corridor and Junction Items
Stairs Hotspots | Stairs Items | Pit Items

Introduction

With level 70 Construction comes the ability to make your very own dungeon and challenge your friends to get through it. (Challenge and PvP Challenge modes can be activated using the lever in your throne room if you've built one.) There are 3 room types: corridor, junction and stairs. All of these rooms are contained in this article.

Key Features

A typical dungeon corridor
Corridor
The corridor is a passage that players follow deeper into your dungeon. It contains a variety of hotspots for you to set up fiendish traps to stop or slow down players' progress in their search for your treasure room.

Junction
Very similar to the corridor in terms of hotspots, the junction gives the option to build corridors in a different direction, or to build many to confuse players in the dungeon!

Stairs
Here you can build stairs back out to your house. It is a very similar room to the junction with regards to layout, but this room offers the choice of stairs.

Pits
These rooms are able to contain traps and monsters, but can only be escaped once all the creatures within them have been killed. The host can leave whenever they choose.
